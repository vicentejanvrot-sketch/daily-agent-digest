# Daily Agent Digest

## Your AI-Powered Research Assistant

Daily Agent Digest keeps you on top of the videos that matter — without the endless scrolling. Set up AI agents to monitor your favorite channels, and let them surface what's actually worth watching.

---

## What It Does

### Monitor Channels Automatically
Create AI research agents, assign channels, and they'll scan for new videos on a schedule. No manual checking — your agents do the work.

### Build a Smart Research Feed
Every new video your agents discover lands in your Feed. Mark videos as Watched, Liked, or Watch Later to stay organized. A progress bar on each feed shows how much you've worked through.

### AI-Powered Video Analysis
Bring your own AI keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini) and let agents enrich videos with summaries, insights, and analysis. Great for researchers, analysts, and power users who need more than just a thumbnail.

### Connect Your YouTube Account
Link your YouTube account to sync your playlists, watch history, and video actions. Actions you take in the app (Watched, Liked, Watch Later) sync back to YouTube.

### Watch Videos In-App
A built-in video player with quality controls, playback speed, and fullscreen support. No need to leave the app.

### Track Your Watch Time
Detailed watch time statistics so you can see how you're spending time across agents and channels.

### Dashboard at a Glance
See active agents, channels tracked, recent runs, and success rate — all on one screen.

---

## Screens

- **Dashboard** — Overview stats, your feeds with progress bars, and quick "Run Now / Run All" buttons for each agent.
- **Agents** — Manage your AI research agents: create, edit, run, or delete.
- **Feed** — Browse your research feed, filter by status (Unwatched, Watched, Watch Later, Liked), and tap any video to play it.
- **History** — Past runs and what each agent discovered.
- **Settings** — Configure API keys (YouTube Data, OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini), default email, video quality, keep-screen-on toggle, YouTube account connection, and account deletion.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Sign in** with your email.
2. **Add API keys** in Settings: at minimum, a YouTube Data API key so agents can fetch channel/video data.
3. **Create an agent** — give it a name, pick YouTube channels to monitor, and optionally add email recipients and research instructions.
4. **Run your agent** — tap "Run Now" and it will scan the channels for new videos.
5. **Browse your Feed** — watch videos, mark them Watched or Watch Later.

---

## Requirements

- An active internet connection.
- A YouTube Data API key (free tier available from Google Cloud Console).
- Optional: OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini API keys for AI-powered video analysis.

---

## Privacy

Your data is stored securely and never sold. API keys are write-only — they're never displayed back or read after saving. You can delete your account and all data at any time from Settings.
