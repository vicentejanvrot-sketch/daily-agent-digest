# Daily Agent Digest — Support

Need help with **Daily Agent Digest**? We're here for you.

## Contact

- **Email:** support@travelone.ca

We aim to respond to all support requests within 1–2 business days.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I create an account?
Open the app and tap **Create Account**. Enter your email and a password
(at least 6 characters), and you'll be signed in right away.

### What does the app do?
Daily Agent Digest lets you set up smart agents that track the channels and
topics you care about, then delivers a focused daily digest of new videos
along with watch-time stats.

### How do I set up an agent?
From your dashboard, create a new agent and choose the channels or topics you
want it to track. Your agent will gather new content and include it in your
daily digest.

### I forgot my password. What do I do?
On the sign-in screen, tap **Forgot password** and follow the instructions
sent to your email.

### How do I delete my account or data?
Email us at **support@travelone.ca** with your request and we'll process it
promptly.

## Reporting a Problem

If something isn't working, please email **support@travelone.ca** with:

- The device and OS version you're using
- A short description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce it (if known)
- A screenshot, if available
